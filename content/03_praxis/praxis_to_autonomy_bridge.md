## 1. Operational Definition
This bridge manages the validation gates, failure signals, and sovereignty milestones required to transition a learner from AI-assisted sparring (Praxis) to autonomous human reflection and hybrid integration (Autonomy).

## 2. Theoretical Foundation
Draws from metacognition theory, self-regulated learning (Zimmerman), and epistemic agency concepts, ensuring the user moves from structured dialectic sparring to independent philosophical evaluation.

## 3. Cartesian Axis Mapping
- **Ontogeny Coordinate ($X$):** 6.0 (Advanced conceptual reasoning)
- **Praxis Coordinate ($Y$):** 5.0 (Sustained dialectic conflict)
- **Autonomy Coordinate ($Z$):** 5.0 (Transitioning to user self-regulation)

## 4. Core Mechanics / Dynamics
1. Tracks the user's interaction logs for signs of self-correction and independent study direction.
2. Slowly diminishes automated prompts, forcing the user to formulate their own Socratic guidelines and structure their own inquiries.
3. The bridge is crossed when the learner can successfully audit their own dialogue history and document their personal cognitive growth path without AI facilitation.

## 6. Transition Gates / Failure Signals
### Entry Gates
* **Sparring Mastery:** Successful completion of 5 separate sparring cycles with the `pattern_perturbator` or other dialogue protocols (Praxis index $\ge$ 5.0).
* **Bias Awareness:** Consistent documentation and flagging of machine-introduced fallacies or biases.

### Exit Gates
* **Independent Metacognition:** Learner produces a self-audited log detailing their cognitive biases and shifts in opinion over time (Autonomy index $\ge$ 7.0).
* **Self-Directed Prompts:** User defines their own custom constraints and roles for the AI, rather than relying on system defaults.

### Failure Signals
* **Prompt Dependency:** The user is unable to structure a topic or direct an inquiry without asking the AI how to do it.
* **Consensus Convergence:** The user alters their own initially correct hypotheses to match the AI's standard biases when challenged, showing a lack of independent verification.
* **Audit Evasion:** Total failure or refusal to review, comment on, or edit past dialogue logs (zero metacognitive reflection).

## 7. Cognitive Risk Mitigations
- Mitigates: [[epistemic_dependency]]
- Mechanism of defense: Progressively disables active AI tutoring interfaces, turning the machine into a passive dictionary/factual lookup device to prevent dependence.

## 8. Anti-Patterns
- **The Echo Chamber:** Believing that co-agency sparring is autonomous work, when the user is simply following the rails set by the AI's structural scaffolding.

## 9. Observable Markers
- User initiates 90% of structural changes in the topic map.
- User writes extensive, standalone journal reflections that do not feed back into the AI loop.
