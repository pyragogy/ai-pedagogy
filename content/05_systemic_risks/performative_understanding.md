## 1. Operational Definition
Performative Understanding is the feeling of competence that arises when reading a flawless summary or schema, which hides the fact that the reader cannot reconstruct the material or apply it to a new domain.

## 2. Theoretical Foundation
Based on educational research regarding the "illusion of knowing" and the difference between recognition memory (easy) and recall memory (hard).

## 3. Cartesian Axis Mapping
- **Ontogeny Coordinate ($X$):** 4.0
- **Praxis Coordinate ($Y$):** 1.0 (Low effort)
- **Autonomy Coordinate ($Z$):** 3.0

## 4. Core Mechanics / Dynamics
1. The user requests a summary of a difficult paper.
2. The AI returns a bulleted summary.
3. The summary is highly readable and logical, giving the user an instant sense of comprehension.
4. When asked to apply the concepts to a new problem without looking at the text, the user fails completely because they never performed the cognitive labor of synthesis.

## 8. Anti-Patterns
- **The Bullet-Point Scholar:** Believing that because you have stored a library of 100 AI-generated summaries, you have acquired the knowledge contained in those papers.

## 9. Observable Markers
- Quick reading times on complex summaries.
- Extreme difficulty or failure when asked to explain the concept in plain words without reference to the original bullets.
