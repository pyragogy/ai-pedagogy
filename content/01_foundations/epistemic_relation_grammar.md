## 1. Operational Definition
The Epistemic Relation Grammar defines the semantic linkages allowed in frontmatter metadata to map structural knowledge configurations.

## 2. Theoretical Foundation

## 4. Core Mechanics / Dynamics
The syntax defines five directional link types:
1. `requires`: A dependency constraint indicating the target must be operational first.
2. `reinforces`: A support relationship increasing the semantic weight of both nodes.
3. `contradicts`: A conflict mapping signifying opposing principles.
4. `mitigates`: A safety link where the source reduces the inherent risks of the target.
5. `triggers`: An execution link initiating a transition or protocol.
